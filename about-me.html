My Mission: Since learning about the biases in AI, when it comes to gender and race, in APCSP, I've wanted to learn more about the issue and spread awareness. As a woman in tech, I know of the racism and sexism in the field. When I talked to people about the issue, they didn't even realize it. So many of us overlook the problem in artificial intelligence with how much it hides behind the "I'm super-advanced" and "I could do no wrong" personas. AI being held accountable and inspected makes way for a safer technological future and erases the possibility of digital dystopia. No matter how big the impact of my mission is, I want to spread awareness and my message. Just knowing about the issue prevents misunderstanding. Please join me on this mission - Mily <3
